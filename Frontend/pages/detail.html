Description

Modernized and characteristic mansion with a deep and sunny backyard, shed and back entrance. Beautifully located within walking distance of the village center and parcs, in the vicinity of the American School of The Hague. Very spacious and bright family house with an extension at the rear, open kitchen, 5 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. Are you looking for a spacious home, located in a good area, in the center of Wassenaar? Then this house may be suitable for you! Layout: Ground floor: entrance, hall with wardrobe and meter cupboard, draft separation to hall with guest toilet, toilet, access to basement, wide living/dining room with parquet floor and fireplace, patio doors to the landscaped backyard, modern open kitchen with an island, gas stove and side by side cooler/freezer, separate utility room with washer/dryer space. Closet with storage space and central heating boiler (Vaillant, 2011). First floor: wide landing with toilet and storage room, master bedroom with fitted wardrobes and a view of the Zijlwetering, rear bedroom with balcony, front bedroom with fitted wardrobes and balcony, modern bathroom with shower, washbasin and access to balcony. Second floor: landing, rear bedroom with dormer window, front bedroom with dormer window, separate storage room at the front, modern second bathroom with shower, toilet and washbasin. Cellar available for storage and a shed in the backyard
